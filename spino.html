SPINOSAURUS
(SPINE-uh-SORE-us)

  • Size: 11-23 Ft. Tall / 50 Ft. Long
  • Weight: 22,000 Lbs. / 9,980 Kg
  • Diet: Carnivore
  • Feature: Semi-aquatic

Spinosaurus Skeleton

DID YOU KNOW?

  • • Triceratops' head was its most imposing feature. It measured 4 to 5 feet (1.2 to 1.5 meters) across and was ornamented with impressive horns and a head plate.
  • • It used its horns -- a short one above its mouth and two long ones above its eyes -- to charge predators, such as T. rex.
  • • The skull of triceratops was one-third the length of its entire body
  • • It lived around 65 million years ago, during what is called the Cretaceous Period.
